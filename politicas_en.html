Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

When making reservations or contacting us, we collect personal data such as name, email, telephone number, and information necessary for the provision of tourism services.

2. Use of Information

The collected data is used to confirm reservations and payments, contact you about tours and services contracted, send relevant information, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing

Your data will not be sold or shared with third parties, except for partners directly involved in the execution of tours or competent authorities when required by law.

4. Storage and Security

The data is stored in secure systems, with technical and administrative measures to prevent unauthorized access, and maintained only for the necessary period.

5. Customer Rights

You can request access, correction or deletion of your data, revoke consent for marketing communications and request data portability in accordance with the LGPD (Law No. 13.709/2018).

6. Cookies and Navigation

Our site may use cookies to improve the user experience. You can configure your browser to block or delete cookies, but this may limit some functionalities.

7. Changes to the Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. The most recent version will always be available on our website.

8. Contact

For questions or requests related to privacy and data protection, please contact us by email at contato@compasspipa.com or through the official WhatsApp of the agency.
